# Privacy Policy for Anvil

**Last Updated:** 16 November 2025

## 1. Introduction

Welcome to Anvil. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application.

## 2. Data Collection

**Anvil does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data.** All data entered into the app is stored locally on your device.

## 3. Data Storage

All insights, categories, and app preferences are stored locally on your device using Android's standard app-specific storage. This data is never transmitted over the internet or stored on external servers.

## 4. Permissions

Anvil requires no special permissions. The app uses standard Android storage for local data persistence.

## 5. Third-Party Services

Anvil uses:
- **PostHog Analytics**: For anonymous app usage analytics (no personal data)
- **Firebase Crashlytics**: For crash reporting (anonymous error logs only)

## 6. Data Sharing

Anvil does not share any data with third parties. When you export PDFs or share via Android's share sheet, you control what is shared.

## 7. Data Deletion

You can delete all app data by uninstalling the app or clearing app data from Android Settings.

## 8. Children's Privacy

Anvil is suitable for all ages. We do not collect personal information from anyone, including children.

## 9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy. The "Last Updated" date will reflect changes.

## 11. Your Rights

You have complete control over your data since it's stored locally on your device.
